Guides for Proper Use of Fintrackr

How does registration work?

If you do not have a registered account, the "Register User" button takes us to the next screen, where the data of a new client is registered.

How does the daily book work?

This is the first screen within the application, with four important sections:
1. Sidebar of options: navigate to different screens.
2. Savings: quick access to the savings registration function.
3. Pending Payments: Sector that allows viewing and adding pending transactions and then marking them as completed, to maintain organization.
4. Budgets: Direct access to the function of adding budgets for different functionalities

Clicking the "Add" button in the Daily Book section or in the Savings section takes us to the screen where the information to be registered is entered.

Within the sidebar is the Daily Book screen, in this section, transactions registered are detailed, separated by category.

How do Savings and Budgets work?

In the next section of the sidebar, access is gained to Savings and Budgets. In savings, it is possible to update the amount saved and reverse (or delete) the savings so that it can be stored again in the system as income.

Clicking on Reverse opens the following screen to confirm the transferred data.
